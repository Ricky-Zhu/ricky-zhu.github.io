About me

I am a first-year PhD student with Prof. Oya Celiktutan as my supervisor at SAIR lab, King's College London. Prior to that, I received my M.S. degree from Imperial College London working with Prof. Benny Lo. Currently My research interests focus on the application of deep reinforcement learning on real-world robotic scenarios and human-robot interaction. I am also interested in advancing deep reinforcement learning algorithms, such as hierarchical reinforcement learning, offline reinforcement learning.
