Ruiqi Zhu

Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics

King's College London

My name is Ruiqi Zhu (朱瑞琦). I am a first-year PhD student with Oya Celiktutan as my supervisor at SAIR lab, King's College London. Prior to that, I received my M.S. degree from Imperial College London working with Benny Lo. Currently My research interests focus on the application of deep reinforcement learning on real-world robotic scenarios and human-robot interaction. I am also interested in advancing deep reinforcement learning algorithms, such as hierarchical reinforcement learning, offline reinforcement learning.
